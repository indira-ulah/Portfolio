About the Project

TORCH: Trusted Platform for Custom Art Services is a cutting-edge digital platform designed to connect artists with clients seeking custom artwork. The platform serves as a trusted marketplace for digital illustrations, hand-painted portraits, graphic designs, and other personalized art commissions. TORCH focuses on providing a seamless and secure environment where clients can find and collaborate with artists, ensuring quality, transparency, and trust in every transaction. The platform aims to empower artists by providing them with a space to showcase their talent, manage their portfolios, and interact directly with clients, all while ensuring they receive fair compensation for their work.
